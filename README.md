# Afrorama

Empowering Afro-optimists with opportunities, visibility, and community.

## About

Afrorama is a platform that equips Afro-optimists with the resources to contribute to Africa's development. First launched on LinkedIn by Chloé Bertrand on 13 October 2021, Afrorama has grown into a community-driven space for impact-focused professionals.

## What We Do

- **Opportunities** — We connect talent with Africa-based and Africa-related opportunities in the impact economy, spanning jobs, volunteer roles, consultancies, research positions, training, and funding.
- **Career Support** — We help job seekers gain insight into the job market, access development resources, and get support with job applications.
- **Community** — We cultivate a space for networking and peer learning, so you don't have to figure it all out alone.

## Who We're For

Afro-optimists — people who believe in Africa's potential and want to actively contribute to its development.
